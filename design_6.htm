Meet the HBCWeb Team

HBCWeb is comprised of ultra-talented and experienced designers, developers and programmers. Learn more about each person by reading their descriptions below: (yes it's currently all dudes...)

Rob oversees the day-to-day operations of HBCWeb. From the initial client interactions, to project planning, to the design phase and into development. Rob is also responsible for some design work seen in our Portfolio including HBCWeb.com.

Glenn Generao - Graphic Designer

Glenn is our longest-running employee, having been hired in December of 2004. Glenn is responsible for all thing graphical, his unique and creative approach to design has been a key to establishing HBCWeb as a web design powerhouse.

Wes Sayas - Lead Programmer
